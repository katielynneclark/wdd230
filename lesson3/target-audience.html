City of Bakersfield Chamber of Commerce

Target Audience:

The target audience is for men and women that are ages 25-60. Some of these people love to shop at the Valley Plaza Mall while others love going out to Hart Park to enjoy the outdoors when it is not too hot outside. Some individuals own their own companies while others are employees of others. Many of these people were raised in Bakersfield and will raise their families their as well. The average income for Bakersfield is about $60,000 a year but can go as low as $15,000 and as high as $150,000 per year.

Personas

James is a man in his 30s that lives in Bakersfield with his wife and 2 little boys. James was raised in Bakersfield and loves being outdoors and showing his kids what he did growing up.

Christina is a 45 year old woman that has lived in Bakersfield for about 20 years. She moved there when she got married to her husband, a Bakersfield native to be closer to his family. She loves to read, work in her garden, and spend time with family.
