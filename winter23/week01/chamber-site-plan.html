Bakersfield Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Bakersfield Chamber of Commerce takes on the role of

the city.It also promotes local businesses and can help offer

suggestions of possible activites or places that people may

to go. We strive to maintain the integrity of the city of Bakersfield!
